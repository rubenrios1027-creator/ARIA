*   **How it works:** The attacker establishes a rapport first. They might reference a project the victim is known to be working on, or use jargon from the victim's industry.
*   **Effect:** By appearing helpful and familiar, the attacker builds a false sense of trust, making the subsequent bad request seem like a logical continuation of a conversation.

### 4. Scarcity and Curiosity
*   **How it works:** Appeals to the victim’s desire for exclusive information ("Click this link to see the bonus data") or the fear of missing out.
*   **Effect:** These psychological lures draw the victim into clicking the malicious link or opening the malicious attachment.
